Liege Waffles

Home

Description

This recipe is for creating the best waffles using swedish pearl sugar.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Whole Milk
  • Salted Butter
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Swedish Pearl Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Salt.

Directions

Dough

  1. In a mixer bowl, add 4 cups flour leveled.
  2. Stir in 3/4 tsp cinnamon and 2 and 1/4 tsp salt.
  3. Make a well in the center of the flour.
  4. In a gglass measuring cup, add 1 cup whole milk.
  5. Heat in the microwave for about 30 seconds and stir.
  6. Make sure it is lukewarm but not hot.
  7. Add warm milk to the well in the center of the flour.
  8. Add 1 and 1/2 tsp active dry yeast and 2 tblspn sugar.
  9. Stir the liquid wiht a small spoon and let rest for 5 minnutes.
  10. After 5 minutes the yeast should be frothy.
  11. Crack 2 eggs into the milk.
  12. Break the eggs and lightly whisk into the milk with a fork.
  13. Add 1 tblspn vanila the the liquid.
  14. Use a spoon to stir the flour and liquid together.
  15. Switch to a dough hook and knead until dough comes together completely.
  16. With mixer running on low, add the butter 1 tblspn at a time.
  17. Once butter is incorporated, continue kneading the dough with the hook for 3 minutes.
  18. Scrape the dough down and cover the bowl with plastic wrap.
  19. Place in a warm spot and let rest for 30 minutes.
  20. Mix in pearl sugar and divide dough.
  21. Place dough portions on a greased baking sheet, cover and let rest 1 hour.
  22. Dust each portion before cooking on greased waffle iron for 3-4 minutes.
