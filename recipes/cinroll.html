Cinnamon Rolls

Home

Description

This recipe is for creating the best and gooey-est yeast based cinnamon rolls you've ever had.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt
  • Whole Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Cream Cheese
  • Confectioner's Sugar
  • Vanilla

Directions

Dough

  1. Mix dry ingredients(flour, sugar, salt).
  2. Warm the milk and butter.
  3. Whisk yeast into warm milk and butter until it is dissolved.
  4. Pour yeast mixture over dry mixture.
  5. Add egg and mix everything together.
  6. Transfer newly mixed dough to lightly floured work surface.
  7. Knead by hand for 3 minutes.
  8. Let dough rest for 10 minutes.

Filling

  1. Roll out the dough.
  2. Spread the dough with room temperature butter.
  3. Top the butter with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Roll the dough up.
  5. Cut dough into rolls.
  6. Grease a 9-10inch pan and arrange rolls evenly.
  7. Cover and allow rolls to rise 30 minutes.
  8. Bake rolls at 375F for 25 minutes.

Icing

  1. Beat room temperature cream cheese until smooth.
  2. Add butter and beat until combined and smooth.
  3. Beat in confectioner's sugar and vanilla until smooth.

Finish the Rolls

  1. Remove rolls from over when golden brown.
  2. Allow rolls to cool.
  3. Spread healthy amount of icing on cinnamon rolls.
  4. Serve and Enjoy.
